How to Keep Cool

Indoor Tips

  1. Insulate house
    2.

    Try covering your windows with drapes/curtains. Or, you could place aluminum foil on the windows to reflect sun rays from entering the inside.

  2. Have air conditioning
    3.

    If there is no air conditioning in the building, leave and go to a public library or community center with AC.

  3. Don't rely solely on fan
    4.

    A fan may help you for a short period, but it is not the most practical way to stay cool.

  4. Limit stove and oven use
    5.

    Stoves and ovens can make a buildingâ€™s overall temperature heat up very quickly and it can be hard to cool the building back down.

  5. Drink plenty of water
    6.

    Cool water can lower the body's internal temperature, so drink lots of it. Also, limit dehydrating drinks such as alchohol.

Outdoor Tips

  1. Wear sunscreen
    2.

    Sunburn affects the body's abiltiy to cool down and can make you dehydrated.

  2. Limit outdoor activity
    3.

    Outdoor activity, especially if it's strenous, can make a person exhausted. So, if you are trying to go outside, it is better to go early in the morning or in the evening.

  3. Wear clothing fit for the temperature
    4.

    Clothes that are loose, light weight, and lightly colored can help you stay cool during the heat.

  4. Stay wet
    5.

    Before going out, try drenching your shirt with cool water, or shower with cool water before and after going out. Cool water is very beneficial in the heat.

  5. Take breaks
    6.

    If being outside becomes too much for the body, rest in shade or seek an air conditioner before returning outdoors.

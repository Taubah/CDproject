What's Happening and What Can We Do About It?

Global Warming

The long-term heating of the Earth's suface is known as global warming. Global warming has been observed since about the 1850s-1900s. Human actions, such as fossil burning affect global warming by trapping greenhouse gas levels into the Earth's atmosphere.

Greenhouse gases absorb the thermal energy from the sun that radiates to the Earth's surfac and prevent that heat from going into space. Greenhouse gases are helpful because they sustain life on Earth, but too much can make temperatures rise to deadly levels. According to CNN, " the average global temperature reached 17.01 degrees Celsius (62.62 Fahrenheit)" on July 4, 2023.

How Can We Help Our Planet

All of us can try ways to slow and stop the effects of global warming. Some possible actions we can make are using more renewable energy supporting business that promote sustainable practices. Also, we can try to carpool, bike, and use public transportation when possible.
